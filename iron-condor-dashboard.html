Hover any point on the Grid Tape above to snapshot this panel at that tick. Empty = before trading hours or after EOD flatten.
Strategy & engine live in iron-condor-engine.js (shared with the Node CLI iron-condor-dashboard.mjs). Assumptions: 15-pt wings · entries 09:31–15:45 ET · no profit-taking (positions roll until EOD) · $0.05/contract slippage on every fill (ask to buy, bid to sell) · $100 multiplier. When a new structure's leg exactly offsets an older still-open structure's leg at the same strike, it's treated as a real closing trade on that older leg (realized immediately) rather than a fresh credit — shown as reason "NET" and tagged in the expanded leg detail. P&L is only fully reconciled at EOD when the entire book is flattened. Edit PARAMS below to change any of these.
